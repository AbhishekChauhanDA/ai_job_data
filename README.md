# ai_job_data
To gain insights into how the Artificial Intelligence job landscape is evolving by analyzing:
- Which AI roles are most in-demand
- How experience level impacts salary
- Where the top hiring hubs are located
- What skills are most commonly required

---

##  Dashboard Highlights

- **Salary by Experience Level**: Understand how compensation scales from entry to senior roles.
- **Remote Work Trends**: Breakdown of jobs by remote ratio (0%, 50%, 100%).
- **Top Companies Hiring**: Know which companies are leading AI recruitment.
- **Job Role Distribution**: View which titles are most common (e.g., Data Scientist, ML Engineer).
- **Required Skills**: Explore frequently mentioned tools/languages like Python, SQL.
